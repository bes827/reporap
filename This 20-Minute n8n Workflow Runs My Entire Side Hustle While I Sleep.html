Primary Tools & Functions
  • Tally Form: Lead capture (free/premium users).
  • n8n: Automation brain; orchestrates entire workflow.
  • Gumroad: Digital product delivery; Stripe payment verification.
  • Gmail API: Sends personalized confirmation/product emails.
  • Google Sheets: Simple lead tracking; CRM for retargeting/feedback.
Optional Tool
  • Notion: Manages content; can be used for optional lead tagging.
The 20-Minute Workflow Steps
  • Sequential steps defining the automation logic.
  • Designed for efficiency & user experience.
  • Built in ≈20 minutes, runs autonomously.
Step 1: Trigger
  • User fills Tally form.
  • Purpose: Download free version / Buy premium template.
  • Action: Hits an n8n webhook, initiating workflow.
Step 2: Check Payment Status
  • Conditional logic based on user action.
  • If free user: Email w/ free link sent.
  • If paid user: n8n checks Stripe payment via Gumroad webhook; once verified, thank-you email + product link sent.
Step 3: Add to CRM (Google Sheet)
  • Every lead (free/paid) logged into Google Sheet.
  • Purpose: Retarget free users w/ discount; Get feedback from paid users; Spot buying patterns.
Step 4: Deliver Product
  • Personalized email sent from author's Gmail.
  • Content: User's name, template access link, bonus Loom video for onboarding.
  • Effect: Feels human, but fully automated.
