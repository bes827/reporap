Idea #1: Sell Prompt Packs
  • Prompts are new templates; people pay for time-saving bundles.
  • Digital product w/ zero inventory → infinite sales.
  • Price range: $5–$25 per pack.
Mechanism
  • Create collections of effective prompts for specific tasks.
  • Sell these curated bundles as digital products.
Tools
  • ChatGPT: Generate prompts.
  • Canva: Design covers/mockups.
  • Gumroad/Etsy: Sell digital products.
Example
  • Social Media Caption Pack: 50 prompts for Instagram creators.
  • LinkedIn Outreach Prompts Pack: Created in 1 afternoon, covered groceries within a month @ $9/pack.
