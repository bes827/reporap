Writing the NIH Grant Proposal: Preparation and Preliminary Steps
  • Source: Gerin et al., 2018
  • Topic: Grant Preparation
  • Author(s): Gerin, Kapelewski Kinkade, Page
1. Readiness Assessment
  • Possess PhD/MD credentials and core substantive knowledge.
  • Have mentors/sponsors lined up for training grants.
  • Secure permission from department chair/division chief.
  • Set up eRA Commons account for electronic submission.
2. Eligibility Criteria
  • General rule: Individuals w/ skills, knowledge, resources.
  • Underrepresented groups/disabilities encouraged.
  • Citizenship req for T/F/K awards: U.S. citizen, non-citizen national, or permanent resident (I-551).
